Work Process

I will provide a monthly report detailing the student's progress for all sessions held in the month. All sessions will proceed once proof of payment is received. All refunds will be processed at the end of the month when they are due. Lesson cancellations must be sent at least a day before the lesson date. ​ Students are expected to behave professionally and respectfully. All concerns are to be raised as soon as they arise to ensure optimum benefit from each session. Additional study resources are provided based on students' needs and availability.

High School

Tutoring methods deployed will depend on the student's preferred way of learning. I train students to be able to answer questions, thus all students irrespective of their chosen learning style will be the ones doing the work while I provide guidance. From some sessions, there will be homework from the work discussed, to keep them busy until our next meeting.

Tertiary

Students must provide the course outline of the module they want to book a tutorial session for. I put this group of students under pressure, as one needs to understand that tertiary assessments are designed such that mostly the focused and confident candidates pass. My responsibility is to ensure that as a resource, I give students all the tools needed to make their assessment experience better.
